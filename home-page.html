Notification: Registration schedule for Spring-2023

As per the Academic Calendar, the Pre-Registration and Registration schedule is as follows;

  1. You must pay BDT 15000/- before confirming the registration.
  2. January 01-11, 2023: Registration and student advising.
  3. Up to January 21, 2023: Course withdrawal/Add/Drop with 100% adjustment.
  4. January 22, 2023: Registration confirmation without fine.
  5. January 23-31, 2023: Registration confirmation with late fine BDT 100/- per day.
  6. February 01-13, 2023: Registration confirmation with late fine BDT 5000/- .
  7. February 14, 2023: Registration closed with all kinds of late fines ( No registration will be done after this date).
  8. February 28, 2023: 2nd payment, 50% of the rest amount.
  9. April 05, 2023: 3rd payment, remaining amount.

Student can pay their tuition fees through;

  1. Online: Go to MIU Portal> Home> My Report> My Billing History> Pay Online>>
  2. CellFin (IBBL): Home> Bill Pay> Education Fees> Manarat International University>
